ABOUT


Alan Mendez is a New York City-based software engineer, data scientist, and creative problem solver. Prior to his work in the tech industry, Alan spent four years working as an actor, musician, and composer, where he gained firsthand experience cultivating his skills in collaboration and product development. Alan went on to gain certification in Machine Learning by Stanford University via Coursera, exploring applications ranging from product recommendation systems to statistical modeling methods including linear regression and neural networks. Most recently, Alan was contracted as a web developer with Jen Waldman Studio: Online, where he built and implemented customized HTML and CSS blocks in a beta software built in Bootstrap.

In his free time, Alan enjoys reading and feeding his love of learning. His main side project includes building a mobile web app that connects New York City artists with local rehearsal studio spaces based on real-time availability data, to acquaint arts workers with often overlooked venues and connect studio spaces with additional potential customers. Alan also has extensive knowledge of French and American wines, which he enjoys with cheese of any nationality.
